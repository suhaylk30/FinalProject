The Goal

The goal of this website was to educate users on the how the internet has evolved. The internet has come a long was from just a thought, and through this website, you will be able to see how much has come from just this single thought.

The Audience

This website was made to educate all ages on the topic of the internet. It is very important that we all know specifically where most of our time is spent. It is very surprising once anyone finds out how their favorite apps came from just a thought. Those thoughts were then turned into reality when someone had an idea to make the internet and all it has to offer.

The Topics

The topics of the website include a beginning page where we go back all the way to the start of the internet which was known as the ARPANET. Then we get into Internet Service Providers and programs like Windows. We finally come all the way to the creation of some of out favorite sites like YouTube and Twitter.
